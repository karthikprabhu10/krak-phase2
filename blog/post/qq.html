QQ is a popular messaging app developed by Tencent, widely used in China for instant messaging, voice and video calls, and file sharing. Despite being overshadowed by WeChat globally, QQ remains a versatile app with a strong user base, especially among younger audiences.

What Is QQ?

QQ is a comprehensive messaging and social networking app developed by Tencent. Launched in 1999, it originally gained popularity in China for its instant messaging capabilities but has since evolved to include a wide variety of features. With QQ, users can send text, voice, and video messages, as well as share files, play online games, and stream music. The app also supports large group chats and multi-user video calls. QQ is available on both mobile and desktop platforms, making it versatile for personal use, business communication, and entertainment. While it's similar to WeChat, QQ is particularly popular among younger users in China, offering more entertainment-oriented features.
