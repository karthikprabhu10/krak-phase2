What Is WeChat?

WeChat is a multifunctional social media, messaging, and mobile payment app developed by Tencent. First launched in 2011, it has become a central part of everyday life in China, offering far more than just messaging services. Users can send text, voice, and video messages, make video calls, and share photos through its "Moments" feature. WeChat also integrates a powerful mobile payment system called "WeChat Pay," allowing users to pay for goods and services, transfer money, and even handle bills. Additionally, the app supports mini-programs, which enable various services like booking taxis, shopping, and managing appointments. With its wide range of features, WeChat acts as an all-in-one platform for communication, social interaction, and financial transactions.
